## The Vibe Coding Philosophy

This Christmas reminded me why I love coding:

**No pressure, just progress.** No deadlines, no stakeholders, no meetings. Just me, an editor, and problems I wanted to solve.

**Follow the energy.** Jumped between projects based on what felt interesting in the moment. 175 commits on Lodestar one week, 165 on Windows15 another. The point isn't to finish‚Äîit's to enjoy the process.

**Build tools that build tools.** Both Lodestar and Klondike are meta-tools for working with AI agents. They make my future coding sessions more productive, which means more time for creative work.

**Ship it.** Getting Lodestar documented and published felt great. If code sits on your hard drive, it's just a hobby. Publishing it makes it real.
